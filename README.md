## Research Focus Areas


### 1. Defensive AI for Secure Code
- AI-assisted secure code review and vulnerability discoveries.
- Detection of insecure patterns aligned with CVE's and OWASP Top 10.
- Analyzing Secure by design guidance during the development lifecycle.
- Risks and limitations of AI assisted development tools.


### 2. Defensive AI for Infrastructure Security
- AI-driven anomaly detection across networks, workloads, and cloud systems.
- Configuration risk analysis and privilege escalation discoveries.
- Alert prioritization and signal correlation for SOC operations.


### 3. Threat Modeling AI Systems
- Identification of assets, adversaries, and trust boundaries in AI-driven security tooling.
- Extension of classical threat modeling frameworks (STRIDE, CIA) to AI systems.
- Analysis of AI-specific failure modes, including hallucinations and misuse.


### 4. Incident Response for AI-Driven Security Systems
- Detection and triage of AI misbehavior.
- Containment strategies when AI produces unsafe or incorrect outputs.
- Post-incident analysis and continuous improvement for AI-based defenses.


### 5. Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)
- Mapping defensive AI use cases to regulatory expectations (HIPAA, PCI-DSS and general security controls).
- Accountability, auditability, and documentation of AI driven security decisions.
- Risk management strategies for deploying AI in regulated work environments.

### 6. Identity and Access Management (IAM)
- AI assisted detection of anomalous identity behavior.
- Role and privilege analysis using AI.
- Safeguards for AI systems interacting with identity infrastructure.
